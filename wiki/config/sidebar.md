Brno is the 2nd largest city in Czech Republic, heart of Europe, with almost 400k inhabitants. We have great architecture, universities, technology companies, dependable public transport. The surrounding area - Southern Moravia - offers wineries, cave systems, fairytale castles and magical hills and forests with biking & hiking trails... Come visit us!
